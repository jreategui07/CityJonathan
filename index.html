Margarita Island, Venezuela

Welcome to the pearl of the Caribbean!

About the City.

Margarita Island, known as the pearl of the Caribbean, is celebrated for its beaches, historical castles, and vibrant nightlife. Located in Venezuela's coast, it features beautiful beaches and historic sites. As night falls, the island comes alive with lively bars and nightclubs, offering an exciting mix of relaxation and entertainment.

The most famous attractions in Margarita are:

  1. Beach "El Yaque"
  2. Juan Griego
  3. Bora Bora Disco
